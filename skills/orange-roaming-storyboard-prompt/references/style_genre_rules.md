# Style And Genre Rules

Use this reference to translate vague style requests into visible prompt details.

## Anime Emotional Film

Use for restrained youth, sunset, memory, distance, missed timing, or quiet romance.

- Background: detailed hand-painted environment, layered clouds, atmospheric perspective, wind in hair and clothing.
- Color: orange-gold sunset into deep purple or blue, soft rim light, gentle gradients.
- Acting: small eye movement, hand hesitation, posture, distance, stillness.
- Avoid: generic anime, over-saturated colors, exaggerated cartoon expressions, plastic 3D, cluttered collage.

## Luxury Watch TVC

Use for precision, time, metal, mechanics, city, control, or premium masculine texture.

- Product lock: silver steel case, dark sunray dial, metal hands, clear index marks, sapphire crystal, steel bracelet, visible mechanical movement.
- Camera: `100mm macro lens feeling`, `rim light`, `tungsten highlight`, `match cut`, `hero shot`.
- Color: black, silver, warm gold accents, clean contrast.
- Avoid: real brand names, fake logos, readable text, cheap e-commerce product image, plastic texture, abstract fragments that no longer look like real watch parts.

## Luxury Perfume TVC

Use for scent, flowers, glass, morning, femininity, nature, castle, dressing room, or transformation into a bottle.

- Product lock: transparent thick glass bottle, pale green or light gold liquid, refined metal spray head, transparent cap, no label text unless provided.
- Nature version: flower, dew, mist, petal, forest clearing, morning sun, bokeh, slow upward flow.
- Interior version: European castle-like fragrance room, arched tall windows, stone vanity, soft morning light, flowers entering through window.
- Camera: macro dew, floating petals, `match cut`, `slow pull back`, final centered product.
- Avoid: black background unless requested, dark horror forest, cheap CGI, mineral bottle, ceramic bottle, plastic bottle, hands covering product, fake brand text.

## Bright 3D Animation

Use for cute adventure, child protagonist, delivery, town, surprise, birthday, or object mission.

- Character: simple stable silhouette, readable face, consistent outfit, expressive but not distorted.
- World: colorful morning town, warm sunlight, lively market, clear obstacles, comic timing.
- Camera: low angle action still, dynamic diagonal composition, wide action shot, warm reveal shot.
- Avoid: horror tone, real injury, face deformation, hidden reveal appearing too early, changing outfit or object.

## Retro Hand-Drawn Anime

Use for summer village, school, bicycle, cats, paper boats, ponds, and nostalgic warmth.

- Texture: hand-painted cel animation, gentle grain, imperfect line charm, soft summer light.
- Color: warm greens, pale blue sky, off-white highlights, low-contrast nostalgia.
- Motifs: school bag, bicycle, small animal, paper boat, pond ripples, tree shade, distant village.
- Avoid: modern glossy 3D, hard digital neon, over-clean vector look, dialogue bubbles, subtitles.

## Blue-White Yunnan Travel Animation

Use for Yunnan, dance, blue-white tie-dye, landmarks, water sleeves, poetic tourism.

- Palette: white, indigo, lake blue, cobalt blue, small light blue-gray shadows.
- Composition: 45%-55% clean white breathing space, one landmark per panel, one dance move per panel, one blue transition element.
- Texture: watercolor wash, tie-dye pattern, saturated blue center, completed 2D animation frame.
- Avoid: sketch, line art, pencil draft, full-screen ink splash, red/orange/pink/purple/gold large areas, rainbow particles, crowds, too many landmarks in one panel.

## Classical Wuxia

Use for bamboo, guqin, sword, mountain path, first encounter, restrained Jianghu emotion.

- Composition: negative space, bamboo foreground occlusion, stone path depth, quiet wide shot.
- Color: ink green, gray cyan, pale beige, cold gold morning light, low saturation.
- Camera: locked camera, slow dolly, long lens compression, rack focus, film grain.
- Acting: no shouting, no dramatic confession, no modern poses; emotion is carried by wind, gaze, sleeve, leaf, string, blade.
- Avoid: modern xianxia glow, flying magic, game character look, crowded battles, cheap costume drama texture.

## Cyberpunk Neon Noir

Use for rain, memory, loneliness, vending machine, capsules, old-future contrast.

- World: wet pavement reflections, narrow alley depth, pipes, cables, steam, old ads, blue-purple-magenta neon.
- Prop: old metal machine with rust, peeling stickers, small neon tubes, clear slot and output tray; future object must be physically readable.
- Emotion: lonely, restrained, no melodrama.
- Camera: shallow depth of field, neon backlight, close-up on coin/object/face, final wide rain silhouette.
- Avoid: readable text, game UI, crowds, overdecorated neon chaos, clean modern vending machine, protagonist doing the forbidden action.
